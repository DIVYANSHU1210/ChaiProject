One Nation, Many Chais

Discover the flavours, stories, and warmth of chai from every corner of our country.

The Heartbeat of Every Home

From the snowy valleys of Kashmir to the sunny streets of Kerala, one thing unites us all — a hot cup of chai. It's more than just a drink — it's a part of our daily life, our conversations, and our memories.

Morning Ritual: In one household, chai marks the beginning of each day. It’s made with ginger, cardamom, and lots of love. Everyone gathers in the kitchen, chatting about the day ahead.
Evening Pause: After school and work, the family meets again over chai and snacks. It's the moment everyone waits for — laughter, storytelling, and relaxing together.
Guest Welcome: No guest leaves without having chai. Whether it's masala, lemon, or plain milk chai — it’s the sign of warmth and welcome.

So Many Flavours

Chai changes from place to place — kadak in Mumbai, spiced in Rajasthan, sweet in Bihar, and herbal in the Northeast. Each cup has a new story, a different taste, but the same warmth.
